Presubmission Checklist

  1. If you are new to eCommons, we suggest you review our help pages for submitters.
  2. Identify the collection to which you wish to submit. Request permission to submit to an existing collection if the one you want does not appear in the dropdown menu below by contacting the eCommons Administrators.
  3. Complete the eCommons New Collection Request Form, if a new collection is needed.
  4. Check your content for accessibility, and correct issues. NOTE: accessibility is a requirement.
  5. If you are depositing data, we suggest you contact the Research Data Management Service Group for assistance and consult their information on Data Curation Services and Submission checklist for datasets in eCommons.
  6. Have your metadata and files ready.
  7. Review the eCommons License, and consider whether you want to apply a Creative Commons license to your work.
